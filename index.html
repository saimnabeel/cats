About Cats

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as pets. They belong to the family Felidae and are known for their independence and playful nature.

Cats are known for their agility and playful behavior. They have sharp retractable claws and keen senses, making them excellent hunters.

Did you know? Cats sleep for 70% of their lives!
